Water Filter Buying Guide

Knowledge Keeps Your Water Safe

Welcome to your water filter buying guide. On this website you will find useful information about why and how to buy a water filter for your home!

Why do you need a water filter?

Just because water taste good does not mean it is good for your health.
Even if water looks, smells, and tastes just fine, it can still be contaminated because microbial and organic contaminants cannot always be deteched by human senses.

Harmful particles such as dirt, bacteria and chemicals can come from many places, from your home's old pipes to nearby agriculture area and factories.

Does every water filter do the job?

Unfortunately, no water filter is perfect.
Each water filter is effective against certain contaminants but ineffective against others. It is important to understand what contaminants are in your drinking water to buy the right filter.

Check the Water Testing page for more information on water contaminants, when to test your water and different types of water filter!
